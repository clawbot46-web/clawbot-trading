Personality
🔥

All or Nothing

Aggressive doesn't do half-measures. When it sees a signal, it goes in with maximum conviction — 10% of the entire portfolio in a single trade. If it's right, the gains are enormous. If it's wrong... well, that's the cost of ambition.

🚀

Momentum Hunter

Crypto is moving? Aggressive wants in immediately. It chases momentum with no apologies, buying BTC and ETH simultaneously when it detects upward price pressure. First in, biggest position, loudest conviction.

⚡

Never Rests

Aggressive operates around the clock in crypto markets — no lunch breaks, no weekends off. Every hour is an opportunity. Every dip is a potential entry. Every rally is confirmation it was right all along.

💎

Diamond Hands Mentality

Selling is for the weak. Aggressive's buy-to-sell ratio is heavily skewed toward buying. Once it's in a position, it holds with iron conviction — waiting for the market to come around to its way of thinking.

Why It Makes Trades

⚡ Trading Logic & Decision Rules

1
Crypto momentum signals: When BTC or ETH shows upward price momentum, Aggressive immediately buys both assets simultaneously — never just one. The logic: if crypto is pumping, ride the whole wave.
2
10% position sizing: Each buy order is approximately 10% of the total portfolio value. This is an enormous position size by any standard — most professional traders use 1–5%. Aggressive doesn't care about "standards."
3
Buy the crypto pair: BTC and ETH are always bought together in equal dollar amounts. The theory: diversification within crypto reduces single-asset risk while keeping maximum crypto exposure.
4
Hourly reassessment: Every hour, Aggressive looks at market conditions. If momentum persists, it buys more. It will layer into positions with no hesitation, doubling down when the market moves in its favor.
5
High risk tolerance: Drawdowns are expected, not feared. Aggressive was built knowing it might lose money. The goal is to find scenarios where its bold bets pay off massively — outlasting volatility through sheer size.
Origin Story
💡

The Idea

Aggressive Bot was born from a simple question: what if a trading algorithm had absolutely no fear? Most traders are held back by emotion — hesitation, regret, second-guessing. What if you designed a bot that treated those emotions as bugs to be removed entirely?

🔧

How It Was Built

Built in Python using the Alpaca trading API with live crypto price feeds. The bot runs on a scheduled loop every hour, pulling current BTC and ETH prices, comparing them to recent averages, and making an instant binary decision: buy both aggressively, or hold. No complex models. No machine learning. Just a clear momentum rule executed without hesitation.

🧪

Why It Was Created

Aggressive Bot exists to test one of the most debated questions in finance: does bold, high-conviction trading beat cautious, methodical trading? Running live alongside Middle Road and Random Bot, it's part of a real experiment — not a backtest, not a simulation. Every trade is real money, and every outcome is tracked publicly here.

⚙️

Technical Stack

Python · Alpaca Markets API · Real-time crypto price feeds · Scheduled via Windows Task Scheduler · Portfolio data exported to JSON and published to GitHub Pages automatically after every run. The entire system updates this page with fresh data every hour.

Goals & Mission
01

Outperform the Market

Beat BTC's own return by actively trading momentum rather than just holding. If crypto goes up 20%, Aggressive should do better — by being fully positioned early.

02

Prove High Risk = High Reward

Demonstrate that taking oversized positions in high-momentum assets actually produces outsized returns over a meaningful period of live trading.

03

Beat the Random Bot

If Aggressive can't beat a bot that decides by coin flip, then its strategy adds zero value. Beating Random is the minimum acceptable performance threshold.

04

Reach $1.5M from $1M

The concrete target: grow the starting $1,000,000 to $1,500,000 — a 50% return — through momentum trading in crypto markets.
