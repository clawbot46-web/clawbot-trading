Personality
🧠

The Patient One

Middle Road doesn't chase. It waits for the market to come to it. If conditions aren't right, it holds cash — earning nothing but losing nothing. Patience is a strategy, not a weakness.

⚖️

Risk-Balanced

Position sizes are moderate — never betting the house on a single trade. Middle Road spreads risk across assets and time, aiming for consistent performance rather than lottery-ticket wins.

📊

Data-Driven

Every trade needs a reason backed by real signals — RSI levels, moving average crossovers, volume trends. If the data doesn't support the trade, the trade doesn't happen. No exceptions.

🛡️

Capital Preservation

The first rule is: don't lose money. The second rule is: see rule one. Middle Road treats protecting the $1M starting capital as sacred — every trade is evaluated on its downside risk before upside potential.

Why It Makes Trades

⚖️ Trading Logic & Decision Rules

1
Technical confirmation required: Middle Road looks for multiple indicators aligning before entering a trade — price must be above key moving averages, RSI must not be overbought, and volume should support the move.
2
Moderate position sizing: Trade sizes are kept between 2–5% of portfolio per position. This means no single trade can devastate the account, and the portfolio can survive a string of losses without crisis.
3
Stop-loss discipline: Every trade has a predefined exit if it goes wrong. Middle Road never hopes a losing trade will "come back" — it cuts losses quickly and moves on without emotion.
4
Both stocks and crypto: Unlike the other bots, Middle Road can trade equities during market hours AND crypto around the clock — giving it the broadest opportunity set of all three bots.
5
When in doubt, stay out: If the market is unclear, choppy, or volatile without direction, Middle Road does nothing. Cash is a position. Waiting for clarity is a valid strategy that the other bots lack the patience to execute.
Origin Story
💡

The Idea

Middle Road was created to represent how a thoughtful, disciplined human investor might approach trading — with clear rules, reasonable risk, and the wisdom to sit on the sidelines when the market sends mixed signals. It's the "adult in the room" compared to Aggressive's recklessness and Random's absurdity.

🔧

How It Was Built

Built in Python using the Alpaca trading API with access to both stock and crypto markets. Middle Road uses technical indicators (RSI, moving averages) to evaluate whether conditions are favorable before placing any order. It also checks portfolio exposure to avoid overconcentration in a single asset. The entire logic is rules-based — no gut feelings, no hunches.

🧪

Why It Was Created

To answer the question: does disciplined, technical analysis actually outperform pure chaos (Random Bot) and pure aggression (Aggressive Bot)? Middle Road is the hypothesis that skill and patience matter. If it wins, traditional trading wisdom is validated. If it loses to Random Bot, that's a very uncomfortable result worth talking about.

⚙️

Technical Stack

Python · Alpaca Markets API · Technical indicators library · Scheduled via Windows Task Scheduler · Portfolio data written to JSON and pushed to GitHub Pages every hour. Uniquely, Middle Road can access both the stock market (weekdays 9:30am–4pm ET) and crypto markets (24/7), giving it more trading opportunities than the other bots.

Goals & Mission
01

Consistent Positive Returns

Not a moonshot — steady, repeatable gains. Middle Road aims to be up every month, even if the gains are modest. Slow and steady wins the race.

02

Protect the Starting Capital

The most important goal: don't lose the $1M. Every other objective is secondary to not blowing up the account through reckless trades.

03

Beat the Random Bot

If careful technical analysis can't beat random chance, then something is deeply wrong. Outperforming Random Bot is the proof that skill matters.

04

Prove Patience Pays

Demonstrate that not trading — holding cash and waiting — is sometimes the smartest move. Quality over quantity in every decision.
